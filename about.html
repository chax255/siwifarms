ABOUT SIWIFARMS

Siwi Farms is a female locally owned organization that majors in farming of rice at Ruvu, we employ over 200 people who are locals solving employment problem and helping to feed the world. our farming methods are modern and assures delivery of high quality rice to the Tanzanian market and the world at large.
